Olfactory Architecture

The Composition

Crafted by Olivier Polge in 2018, the Parfum concentration distills the essence of Bleu de Chanel to its most intense and enduring form — a woody aromatic signature built for those who refuse to compromise.

  • Top NotesCitrus, Labdanum, Mint, Pink Pepper
  • Heart NotesGinger, Nutmeg, Jasmine, Iso E Super
  • Base NotesIncense, Vetiver, Cedar, Sandalwood, White Musk
  • SillageLong-lasting, 8–12 hours projection
  • ConcentrationParfum — 20–30% aromatic compounds
